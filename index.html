In recent years, artificial intelligence has transformed from a sci-fi concept into a tangible reality that affects our daily lives. This post explores the fundamental principles behind neural networks and their impact on modern technology.

Understanding Neural Networks

Neural networks are computing systems vaguely inspired by the biological neural networks that constitute animal brains. The concept has evolved significantly since its inception, leading to breakthrough applications in various fields.

"The development of artificial intelligence could be the biggest event in human history. It might also be the last, unless we learn how to avoid the risks."
